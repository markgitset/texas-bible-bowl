## About the Scholarship

The Mary Orbison Memorial Scholarship honors a beloved Bible Bowl advocate who spent
**40 years** involved in biblical education — serving as a coach, participant, and
test author for Texas Bible Bowl from 2010–2022.

Following her passing in 2022, her family established this award to continue her mission
of supporting young people pursuing biblical knowledge. Texas Bible Bowl honored Mary
with the **Heart of Bible Bowl award in 2022**, recognizing her "attention to detail
and Biblical accuracy."
