## Our Translation Choice

Texas Bible Bowl uses the **English Standard Version (ESV)** for all competition materials.

## Why Not the NIV?

The NIV cannot be reproduced in bulk due to copyright restrictions, which makes it
impractical for a competition that requires printing and distributing large amounts of
Bible text to participants.

## Why the ESV?

The ESV uses an **essentially literal** translation philosophy, striving to "stay as close
as possible to the original wording and style of the biblical authors."

This approach offers several advantages for serious study:

- Maintains **consistent English terminology** for original-language concepts
- **Aligns Old Testament passages** cited in the New Testament
- Preserves the richness and nuance of original meanings

For a competition where careful study and understanding of Scripture are key, the ESV is
an excellent choice. It is a newer translation and very readable.
