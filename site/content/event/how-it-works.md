## Who Can Participate?

Texas Bible Bowl welcomes students from **3rd grade through adults** (up to 115 years old!).
While the name says "Texas," participants from anywhere are welcome.

## Bible Translation

The competition uses the **English Standard Version (ESV)**. The NIV cannot be reproduced
in bulk due to copyright restrictions. The ESV is newer, very readable, and an excellent
choice for competition study. See [Why the ESV?](/event/why-esv/) for details.

## Test Format

Testing occurs **Saturday morning** and lasts approximately 3 hours. The exam consists
of five rounds plus a Power Round:

| Round | Format | Questions | Time | Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Power Round | Closed Bible | Varies | 25 min | 50 |
| Round 1: Find the Verse | Open Bible | 40 | 25 min | 40 |
| Round 2: Fact Finder | Open Bible (multiple choice) | 40 | 20 min | 40 |
| Round 3: Identification | Open Bible (multiple choice) | 40 | 20 min | 40 |
| Round 4: Know the Chapter – Quotations | Closed Bible | 40 | 15 min | 40 |
| Round 5: Know the Chapter – Headings | Closed Bible | 40 | 10 min | 40 |

- Elementary division (grades 3–6) is exempt from the Power Round
- **Individual maximum:** 250 points (200 for Elementary)
- **Team maximum:** 800 points combined

## Age Divisions

| Division | Grades / Age |
|---|---|
| Elementary | Grades 3–6 |
| Junior | Grades 7–9 |
| Senior | Grades 10–12 |
| Adult | Age 18+ or high school completion |

Each division (except Adult) has **Inexperienced** and **Experienced** subdivisions.

## Awards

- **Individual:** 1st–10th place per division
- **Team:** 1st–10th place (if 12+ teams competing)
- **Awards Ceremony:** Saturday evening after dinner

## Annual Curriculum

One Bible book or passage is studied each year, tested each spring.
