## Bible Text Requirements

**Version:** ESV® Bible (English Standard Version®), 2025 edition

**Acceptable formats:**
- Photocopied or printed copies
- Downloaded digital copies
- Copyrighted printed Bibles

## Highlighting and Note-Taking

### Margin Specifications
- Page margins **must not be more than 1 inch** on all four sides
- No page breaks between chapters (page breaks between books are permitted)
- Headers/footers with book/chapter numbers and page numbers are allowed
- Tabs are permitted for chapter/book location

### Handwritten Notes
- Note-taking of **any kind** is allowed within the 1-inch margins
- Notes must be handwritten **by the text owner** — coaches may not pre-provide them
- Drawings/doodling are permitted within margins if done by the owner

### Restricted Materials
- All supplementary study materials must be **removed before testing**
- Only the Bible text with handwritten notes is permitted during exams

## Age Divisions

| Division | Grades / Age |
|---|---|
| Elementary | Grades 3–6 |
| Junior | Grades 7–9 |
| Senior | Grades 10–12 |
| Adult | Age 18+ or high school completion |

Each division (except Adult) has **Inexperienced** and **Experienced** subdivisions.

## Team Competition

- Junior and Senior divisions are eligible to form teams
- Teams consist of **4 students**, formed before competition starts
- Teams compete based on the **experience level of the most experienced member**
- Students may compete individually at one level and on a team at another

## Testing Rounds and Scoring

| Round | Format | Questions | Time | Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Power Round | Closed Bible | Varies | 25 min | 50 |
| Round 1: Find the Verse | Open Bible | 40 | 25 min | 40 |
| Round 2: Fact Finder | Open Bible (multiple choice) | 40 | 20 min | 40 |
| Round 3: Identification | Open Bible (multiple choice) | 40 | 20 min | 40 |
| Round 4: Know the Chapter – Quotations | Closed Bible | 40 | 15 min | 40 |
| Round 5: Know the Chapter – Headings | Closed Bible | 40 | 10 min | 40 |

- **Individual maximum:** 250 points (Elementary: 200, no Power Round)
- **Team maximum:** 800 points combined

## Testing Procedures

### Prohibited Actions
- Participants **may not consult** at any time during testing
- No study questions during testing
- Only Bible text and footnotes are allowed
- **Sharing answers results in a zero score** for that round

### Seating
- Participants sit at tables away from others on their own team
- You must remain in your seat for the duration of testing
- No movement around the room during test administration
- Breaks are given after the Power Round and after Round 2 only

### Other Rules
- No food in the testing area
- Drinks with lids only — placed on the floor
- All testing in designated rooms only
- No early testing permitted

## Awards

- **Individual:** 1st–10th place per division
- **Team:** 1st–10th place if 12+ teams; top (teams − 2) places if fewer than 12
- **Awards Ceremony:** Saturday evening after dinner
