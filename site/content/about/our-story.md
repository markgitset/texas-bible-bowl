## Who We Are

Texas Bible Bowl is a Bible knowledge competition program established in **2010** to keep
the tradition of Bible Bowl alive and thriving following the conclusion of National Bible
Bowl at ACU.

The organization received initial support from the **Magnolia** and **Lake Houston**
churches of Christ, and has grown to bring youth and congregations together from across
the country each year.

*Making God's Word Important*

## Our Mission

1. **Instill a love for Scripture** among youth
2. **Strengthen fellowship** within congregations
3. **Inspire lifelong biblical study**
