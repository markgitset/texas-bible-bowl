Texas Bible Bowl relies on volunteer support to operate during the weekend event.
We especially welcome adults from participating congregations who are attending but not competing.

## Available Roles

### Test Grader
Works with the Grading Coordinator to hand-grade Power Round and Find the Verse tests,
sort materials, and organize score sheets for return to participants.

### Test Proctor / Monitor
Assists the test facilitator by distributing and collecting assessments, monitoring
test-takers for integrity, and maintaining a peaceful testing environment.

### Sports Assistant
Helps the sports director referee team competitions.
**Key requirement:** A loud voice and the ability to relay instructions and rules to
a large group of people.

### Kitchen Help
Prepares, serves, and cleans up for two meals under the Kitchen Coordinator's direction.

### Tribe Leader
Facilitates group bonding through ice-breaker activities, ensures participation in
team activities and Saturday games, and models enthusiasm and team spirit.
